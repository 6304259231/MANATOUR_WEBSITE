Goa pack

Goa is a haven for water sports like kneeboarding, kite surfing, snorkelling, parasailing, paragliding, diving, and kayaking. The water sports in Goa can be enjoyed at any one of the beaches in the north or the south.

The most famous attraction in Goa is its nightlife. If you are not a part of this, you are not meant for Goa. You can party all night till the dawn and there are several places for this. From beach parties to clubs and pubs, you can go anywhere to find the cool crowd that loves partying
